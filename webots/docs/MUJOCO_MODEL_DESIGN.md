# MuJoCo Model Design

`mujoco_soccer/models/t1_2v2_soccer.xml` is generated by `mujoco_soccer/tools_generate_proxy_model.py`.

The model contains:

- Four T1 visual proxy robots: `T1_BLUE_1`, `T1_BLUE_2`, `T1_RED_1`, `T1_RED_2`.
- Three assisted planar base joints per robot.
- Prefixed native gait joints such as `BLUE1_Left_Hip_Pitch`.
- Eight transparent foot-ball proxy geoms.
- A dynamic soccer ball with `soccer_ball_free`.
- Green field, white lines, center circle segments, goals, and low ball-retaining field walls.

Collision groups keep the model simple:

- The ball collides with the ground, walls, and foot-ball proxies.
- Body, arm, torso, and visual leg geoms do not collide with the ball.
- Foot proxies do not collide with other foot proxies or the ground.
